### Navigation Guidance

* Start with `README.md` for scope and route.
* Use `primer/Recursive_Agency.md` for first-contact framing if entering through “agency” discourse.
* Use `papers/paper1/` for the canonical theory.
* Use `CANON.md` and `CROSSWALK.md` to prevent misread.
